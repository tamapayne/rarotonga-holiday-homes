Te-ropical Te-lightz is a bright and breezy island retreat nestled in the heart of Muri, one of Rarotonga's most sought-after locations. This spacious three-bedroom home offers the perfect blend of comfort and tropical charm, making it ideal for families or groups of friends looking to experience the best of Cook Islands living.

The open-plan living areas flow seamlessly to outdoor spaces, inviting the warm island breeze indoors. Wake up to glimpses of the famous Muri lagoon and spend your days exploring the crystal-clear waters just moments from your doorstep. The fully equipped kitchen makes it easy to prepare meals with fresh local produce from nearby markets.

Situated within walking distance of Muri Beach, local cafes, and the renowned Muri Night Market, Te-ropical Te-lightz puts you at the centre of island activity while still offering a peaceful sanctuary to return to. Whether you're here for adventure or relaxation, this welcoming home provides everything you need for an unforgettable Rarotongan holiday.
