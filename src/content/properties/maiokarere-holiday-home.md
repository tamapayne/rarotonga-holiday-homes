Maiokarere Holiday Home offers a comfortable and private island escape in the laid-back Arorangi area on Rarotonga’s west coast. With two bedrooms and two bathrooms, the home is ideal for couples, friends, or small families seeking space, ease and a true holiday feel.

Designed for relaxed tropical living, the house features modern interiors, a well-equipped kitchen, and inviting indoor-outdoor spaces perfect for unwinding after a day in the sun. Spend your afternoons enjoying the warm island climate and your evenings soaking up the vibrant west-coast sunsets Rarotonga is known for.

Located close to beautiful lagoon beaches, local dining spots and popular island attractions, Maiokarere is a peaceful base from which to explore the island while still enjoying privacy and comfort. Whether you’re here to relax, swim, snorkel or simply slow down, this welcoming holiday home makes it easy to settle into island time.
